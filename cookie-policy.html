Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Plumbing Match Hub uses cookies and related technologies to operate the website and improve the request experience.

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Who This Policy Applies To

This Cookie Policy applies to Plumbing Match Hub, a website operated by SRP Group s.r.o. It explains how cookies, local storage, pixels, analytics tags, and similar technologies may be used when you browse the site, submit a request, or interact with our forms and features.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small data files placed on your browser or device when you visit a website. Some cookies last only while your browser is open, while others remain on your device for a longer period. We may also use comparable technologies, such as local storage, tracking pixels, log files, and analytics scripts.

Why We Use Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • keep core website functions working correctly;
  • remember settings or user preferences where applicable;
  • understand how visitors use the site and which pages perform best;
  • support form performance, traffic analysis, and site security;
  • measure the effectiveness of content, calls to action, and lead-routing features.

Types of Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies help the website function properly. They may support page navigation, menu behavior, session handling, fraud prevention, security features, and form submission reliability.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site, which pages receive traffic, how long users stay on a page, and whether a request flow is working as intended. This information helps us improve the website and the homeowner experience.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies may be used to remember choices, settings, or interface behaviors so the site performs more consistently across visits.

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services embedded or loaded on the site, such as maps, analytics services, or communication tools, may use their own cookies or similar technologies subject to their own policies. For example, embedded map features or third-party scripts may place or read cookies independently of Plumbing Match Hub.

Session and Persistent Cookies

Some cookies are session cookies, meaning they expire when you close your browser. Others are persistent cookies, meaning they remain for a set period or until manually deleted. The lifespan of a cookie depends on its purpose and configuration.

How to Control Cookies

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies through browser settings. You can usually review stored cookies, clear them, or configure your browser to alert you before certain cookies are placed. Please note that disabling cookies may affect some site features, page loading behavior, or form functionality.

Cookie Settings and Future Tools

We may provide a cookie settings or consent tool on the site to help users manage non-essential cookies. If that tool is available, your choices may apply only to the device and browser you are using at that time.

Do Not Track Signals

Because browser-based "Do Not Track" standards are not consistently implemented across platforms and vendors, Plumbing Match Hub does not guarantee that it will respond to every Do Not Track signal in the same way.

Relationship to Our Privacy Policy

Information collected through cookies and related technologies may be linked with other information we collect through the website. Please review our Privacy Policy for additional detail on how information may be used or shared.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect operational, technical, legal, or vendor-related changes. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date on this page.
